== Description ==

TrustSpot is a User Generated Content marketing platform that helps brands generate tons of Product & Site Reviews, Photos, Videos, Q&A and more to increase conversion rates & ultimately boost sales. Our marketing suite allows you to collect, curate, manage, respond and display user content from a single platform. That's why, TrustSpot is utilized by over 21,000 Global Brands. 

Once the extension is installed to your store, as new orders flow in they will automatically be pushed into TrustSpot to start the process. After X days, an email will send to your customers asking for the review. Customers can than write a review within the same email they receive, upload a photo / video and than submit the content. 

The extension will automatically add the TrustSpot reviews area to your product pages as well to lessen the implementation time.

== Installation ==

1. Install TrustSpot Reviews either via the Wordpress.org plugin directory or by uploading the files to your server. 
2. Once the app is installed, you will need to navigate to the TrustSpot menu link on the left side.
3. This settings area controls how TrustSpot works on your site. You'll first need to enter your login credentials. You can obtain these by visiting [TrustSpot](https://trustspot.io/?utm_source=wordpress&amp;utm_medium=description_link&amp;utm_campaign=woocommerce_wordpress_page "TrustSpot") and creating an account with a 7-day Free Trial. 
4. After you've connected saved your login details, you'll next need to customize all of your desired options. 
5. Once finished click on the Save button, and you're all done! 

Be sure to ask our Support Team should you have any questions in getting started with your account.
